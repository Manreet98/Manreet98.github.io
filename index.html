Emergence of OTT Platforms

Over-the-top (OTT) platforms have revolutionized the way people consume content. Services like Netflix, Amazon Prime, and Disney+ provide on-demand access to movies, TV shows, and original content. The shift from traditional cable TV to streaming services offers viewers flexibility and personalized entertainment experiences. With increasing internet penetration, OTT platforms have become a mainstream mode of entertainment, shaping the future of digital media.
